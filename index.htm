Short bio: I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in IST at Penn State. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xinyu Xing. During my Ph.D., I did internships at IBM Watson Research Center (Summer 2021) with Dr. Taesung Lee, Dr. Kevin Eykholt, and Dr. Jiyong Jang, and at Nvidia (Summer 2019) with Dr. José M. Álvarez and Dr. Clément Farabet. Before joining Penn State, I received my master's degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University at March 2017. My research interest lies in ML and Security. I am a recipient of the IBM Fellowship Award (2020-2022), Facebook/Baidu Fellowship Finalist (2020), and ACM CCS Outstanding Paper Award (2018).
